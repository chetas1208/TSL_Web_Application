Project Introduction

 Problem : There are so many Applications & Websites through which people can book their trip, But they have to use multiple Apps & Websites to plan the entire trip. Also not every system is safe & Authentic that people can trust that blindly.  Possible Solutions : 1. To Check all the websites carefully & do proper homework to find out the best one. 2. Hire a tour planner who will check all the possible plans he has & provide the best one. 3. To plan a trip for single destination instead multiple. 4. To develop a system for multiple users through which users can find all the necessary planning for their trip (includes choosing destination, planning, booking, payment). 5. Choose nearby location for travel.  Objective : 1. To make Trip planning, Booking & make this whole process easier. 2. To make best website options available. 3. To make booking & payment facility available online, which make the whole process easier & faster. 4. To develop a system which can be used by every ordinary person who has smart phone & internet.





Project Definition

There are so many websites available on different web browsers. Our project is based on App development & programming. We have divided websites into 3 parts : (i) Travel (ii) Stay (iii) Local. We will design a virtual tour operator(ex. BOT) named TOUR PLANNER that will find out the best & authentic options based on all websites sorted out by us, which will suit the requirements of the user.
